About Me

My name is Mira Kasari and I am currently a Junior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree with a double major in Computer Science B.A. and Communications Studies with a New Media track.

I have always been interested in a field with computers, and being creative, I was excited to learn that there was a creative field in computer science that allowed me to explore my own creative abilites on the computer.
